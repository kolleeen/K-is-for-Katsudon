Katsudon Recipe

Ingredients

Amount (Good for 1 Person)


Pork Loin

100 grams

White Rice

200 grams

Bread Crumbs

15 grams

Frying Oil

Appropriate Amount

Flour

1 tablespoon

Shredded Cabbage

20 grams

Lemon

1 slice

Eggs

2 pieces

Tongkatsu Sauce

Appropriate Amount



How to Make

Step 1:

Season the pork cutlet with pinches of salt and pepper, to begin with. Then, put the flour and bread crumbs in separate bowls.

Step 2:

Meanwhile, beat one egg in another bowl. Pour oil into a pan and heat it.


Step 3:

Roll the pork cutlet into the bowl of flour before dipping it in the beaten egg bowl. After that, cover it with bread crumbs and shake off all excess.

Step 4:

As soon as the oil is hot enough, fry the pork cutlet until it is golden brown. When it is ready, scoop the white rice and put it in a bowl.


Step 5:

Place the pork cutlet on top of the rice together with shredded cabbage. Then, sprinkle with Tonkatsu sauce. Finish it off with a lemon garnish and serve.




Reference: Reyes, L. (2022, September 18). Katsu Don (カツ丼). Food in Japan. https://www.foodinjapan.org/chubu/yamanashi-en/katsu-don/
