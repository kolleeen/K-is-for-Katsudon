About the Author

Hi!

I am Kolleen Francheska P. Lacson, a grade 9 student of PSHS-CLC.

The topic of this magazine, Katsudon, was chosen because it is my favorite food due to its savoriness and delicious flavor. Katsudon is also one of my comfort foods because my father cooks it for me whenever I feel sad, happy, or when I miss the taste of it. I also enjoy eating it with my family every once in a while and order it whenever I eat out with my friends.

Because my father has a catering business, we try to eat out to compare the taste of foods from other restaurants. I am also into Japanese food, which is how I discovered Katsudon. It then came to be my favorite food of all time.


Thank You!
